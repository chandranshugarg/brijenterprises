About Brij Enterprises

With 30+ years of experience, Brij Enterprises is a well renowed name for Iron and Steel Products in Mathura, Vrindavan, Barsana, Kotwan, Kosi Kalan, Chhata, Govardhan, Hodal and other parts of Uttar Pradesh, Haryana and Rajasthan.

For our services and network, we have been consistently awarded by major business houses like TATA, Kamdhenu, etc. We have wide variety of Iron and Steel Products. Our values and customer experience makes us one of the trusted businesses in the region.

We have been delivering products and materials to all the major organizations in our region with customers like Pepsi, Coca Cola, Jindal, etc.
