TMT Bar

TMT stands for Thermo Mechanically Treated. We sell high quality TMT bars which serves as backbone for construction or building needs. TMT bars have a tough external layer and a soft core inside. They are corrosion resistant and have high weldability.

GC Sheet / Color Coated Sheet

GC Sheet refer to Galvanized Corrugated Sheet. GC Sheets and Color Coated Sheets are best suited for economical and long lasting roofing needs. We deals in wide variety of sheets of ranging width, length and thickness. Our high quality sheets are durable in nature and low in maintenance.

HR Sheet / GP Sheet

HR Sheets and GP Sheets of vaying weight and thickness serves as a raw material in many industries and for many engineering applications. GP Sheet is Galvanized Plain Sheet which is manufactured by Zinc - coating and are shining silver in appearance. HR Sheet is a Hot Rolled Sheet cut from hot-rolled coil and are blank in appearance.

MS Pipe / MS Tube

MS stands for Mild Steel. As the pipes and tubes are made from mild steel they can easily be welded and hence are available in various shapes and sizes for pipelining and tubing purposes. These are also best suited in many industrial and engineering applications.

Welded Wire Mesh

The welded wire mesh is a metal wire screen that is made up of low carbon steel wire or stainless steel wire. It is available in various sizes and shapes. It is widely used in agricultural, industrial, transportation, horticultural and food procuring sectors. It is also used in mines, gardening, machine protection and other decorations.

Barbed / Fencing Wire

We sell Galvanized Iron Wire which is a Zinc coated steel wire. Galvanized Wire is used for making Barbed Wire. Commonly known as Fencing Wire, it is used for fencing in residential and commercial spaces.

Structural Products

MS Angle, MS Beam, MS Channel, MS Flat

We sell wide variety of structural products made from Mild Steel. High quality MS Angle, MS Beam, MS Channel, MS Flat, etc from major manufacturers helps in construction and building needs.
